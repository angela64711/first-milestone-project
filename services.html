What I Offer

Prenatal Care

Personalized preparation through three in-depth 2-hour session to help you feel informed, grounded, and confident as you approach birth.

  • Conversations around your pregnancy, fears, wishes, and birth preferences
  • Evidence-based childbirth education (physiology, pain management, positions)
  • Breathing, relaxation, and pelvic floor work
  • Birth rights, advocacy, and birth plan creation
  • Postpartum preparation, including breastfeeding guidance
  • Optional fascia touch for comfort and optimal baby positioning

Birth Support

Continuous emotional and hands-on care from early labor through the first hours after birth, offering comfort, reassurance, and gentle advocacy so you feel safe, supported, and grounded. My role is to help protect your space, support informed decision-making, and center your needs and autonomy throughout birth.

  • On-call support and presence from early labor
  • Hands-on comfort measures (massage, counter-pressure, fascia touch, rebozo)
  • Supportive birth environment (lighting, music, essential oils)
  • Continuous presence until 1 to 2 hours after birth
  • Support during initial breastfeeding

Postpartum Care

During the postpartum period, I offer practical and emotional care to support you as you settle into life with your baby. My visits are shaped around your needs, creating space for rest, recovery, and open conversation as you navigate this transition.
What’s included:

  • Postnatal visits
  • Breastfeeding guidance to support comfort and milk supply
  • Baby care guidance and practical reassurance
  • Emotional support and space to process your birth experience
  • Caring for your baby so you can rest, shower, or take time for yourself
  • Light household help and nourishment support when needed

Fertility Support

A compassionate, evidence-based space for those navigating fertility challenges, while trying to conceive or undergoing IVF — especially when feeling dismissed or unheard by the mainstream system. This package offers emotional support, grounded information, and hands-on care to support your well-being on your journey toward conception and a viable pregnancy.
What’s included:

  • Accompaniment over two months
  • Three in-depth sessions
  • Pelvic bodywork to support relaxation and circulation
  • Evidence-based information tailored to your situation
  • Space to explore emotions, fears, and hopes without judgment
  • Unlimited phone and SMS support during the accompaniment period
